Dropping in Fortnite

Where are the best places to drop in Fortnite?
The best places to drop in Fornite aren't necessarily set locations. Try to drop far away from the path of the Battle Bus to ensure that there will be the least amount of company in your area as possible.
How should I drop when I am playing with other people?
Try to drop within the vicinity of your teammates. If they are dropping at Risky Reels for example, try to land there with them or at a location near by. This makes it easier to back one another up if multiple enemies land at the same location.
Where are the worst places to drop?
If you are a new player, it is best not to drop at either Tilted Towers or Dusty Divot, as these are big areas where lots of people drop every match.
