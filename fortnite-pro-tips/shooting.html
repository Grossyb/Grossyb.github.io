Shooting in Fortnite

  • Shooting is not easy to get used to in Fortnite, and takes a lot of time to master.
  • Use the time before games to practice your shooting accuracy.
  • Use guns like the Assault Rifle when you first start playing because they are easier to use than others (i.e. shotguns, snipers).
